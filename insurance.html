Insurance


Our goal is to help remove the financial barriers so our patients can receive the dental care they need and desire. Many patients have some kind of dental insurance. Most dental insurance plans do not cover 100% of the cost of your treatment. Insured patients will receive cost estimates broken down by covered and not covered costs.

Please bring your insurance card so we may promptly get you a breakdown of your benefits.
